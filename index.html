About Me

Passionate Engineer & Creator

I'm Samuel Gatimu Kariuki, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at Chuka University with a deep passion for electronics, power systems, and technology education.

My journey began at Langa Langa Secondary School where I achieved a B+ in KCSE before pursuing my engineering degree at Chuka University. I believe in sharing knowledge — which is why I create educational videos and write engineering articles.

My goal is to become a professional Electrical & Electronic Engineer who delivers practical technological solutions for Kenya and Africa.
