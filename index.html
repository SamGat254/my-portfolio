About Me

Passionate Engineer & Creator

I'm Samuel Gatimu Kariuki, an Electrical and Electronic Engineering student at Chuka University with a deep passion for electronics, power systems, and technology education.

My journey began at Langa Langa Secondary School where I achieved a B+ in KCSE, then continued volunteering as a mentor before pursuing my engineering degree. I believe in sharing knowledge — which is why I create educational videos and write about engineering concepts.

My goal is to become a professional Electrical & Electronic Engineer who bridges the gap between complex technology and practical solutions for Kenya and Africa.
